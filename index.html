I AM WILLIONAIR

Early Childhood life...
Born William Magwiroto on 20 November at Warren Park Clinic. My father passed away when l was still in my mothers womb, l was 7 months old pregnant to be precise, because my mom had stress l was born a premature baby few weeks after my father passed away. I was named William after him. I Grew up in Kuwadzana 3. Attended my primary school at Kuwadzana 4 Primary School and my High School at Kuwadzana High 1 from form 1 to form 3 before l relocated to Kwekwe to stay with my older brother, where l finished my form 4 at Amaveni secondary school.
Where it started.
I grew up in a Christian family, and as far as l can remember l joined church choir at the age of 4 and l was also a member of the Percussion Band from grande 2 upto grade 5. In grade 6 l joined school choir at Kuwadzana 4 primary school. When l was grade 7 our Choir group won provincial competitions.
